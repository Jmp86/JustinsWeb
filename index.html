Experience

I'm currently the neighbor island Regional Manager for Honolulu Coffee. I've worked at Honolulu Coffee for over 13 years now. I started as a part-time barista which moved into a full time position. From there I became a Barista trainer then eventually a Store Manager. As Honolulu Coffee began to expand I took on a bigger role of Regional Manager overseeing 4 locations spread across 2 islands. I now frequently travel from Maui to the Big Island of Hawaii where 2 of my 4 stores are located. I've learned a lot while working for Honolulu Coffee. I'm a very skilled barista with extensive coffee knowkedge. I've hired, trained, and developed numerous employees and managers. I understand the challenges of living in the middle of the ocean on islands that are separated from main distributers and each other. I directly report to the owners of the company who have taught me invaluable lessons about running a business. I love the company that I work for and people that I work with but feel that it's time for a new adventure.
